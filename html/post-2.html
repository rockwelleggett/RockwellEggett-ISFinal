What lead me to Entrepreneurship?

Disney Imagineer

When I was 5 years old me and my family visited Disneyland in Anaheim, California. While there I visited a room called the "Blue Sky Cellar" that showcases the work of Dinsey Imagineers. They design the parks and everything inside of them. I was instantly inspired and wanted to become one. Why is this important? It was the moment I discoevered what I want to do with my life. Make people happy with what I do.

“Do what you do so well that they will want to see it again and bring their friends.”

Walt Disney beleived in working hard and doing things right. The real mindset of an entrepreneur.

Rockwell Watches

At the age of 7 my Dad decided to start his own Watch Company. He asked my permission to name it after me and Rockwell Wacthes was born. Since then it haws grown into a global business. From watches to sunglasses, sponsoring athletes to running gyms, it is more than a company, it is a lifestyle.
