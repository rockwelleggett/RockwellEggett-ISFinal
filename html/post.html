The T Shirt Room

When I was in high school I was hanging around my Dad's business and found a bunch of old T Shirt printing equipment. I looked up some YouTube videos and figured out how to use them! It was right then that I found my desire to learn how to do new things! I wanted a project to work on so I invited my sister to help me out with it and we made family reunion shirts for our annual Lake Powell trip. We desinged them and printed them out for the family! I decided there was a business oppuritnity here and i got to work. My sister left to serve a mission for our church and I built the compnay from the ground up finding clinets, getting equipment, finding out the best ways to do things and more. When she came home fromm her mission, she helped me for the remainder of high school and then I left for my mission. Currently, she is running the business in Bountiful, Utah while I attend school at BYU.

My next business venture comes in the form of a missionary subscription service. The missionary's parents set up a recurring payment for a new tie every month for their missionary. I am in the beginning stages of the business and we are going to start sending them out this August!
